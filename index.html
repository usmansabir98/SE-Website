MEAN STACK DEVELOPMENT SHORT COURSE

ACM NEDUET Student Chapter held a Mean Stack Course. The course’s main focus was to enable the students to keep up with the emerging IT trends. This course helped the students to grasp the core knowledge of mean stack in an extensive manner. Total Course Duration: 12 hrs. Every Monday & Wed (3 hrs/class). Timings: 11 a.m. to 2 p.m. Fees: Rs. 1000

ADOBE PHOTOSHOP & ILLUSTRATOR WORKSHOP

If you can imagine it, why don't you create it? The most important things are not things. So design experiences and bring the art to the cart. ACM NEDUET Student Chapter held a Photoshop and Illustrator Course. The course’s main focus was to enable them design and implement their ideas graphically. This course helped the students to be able to design front end for web and moble apps as well as continue a career in graphic designing. Total Course Duration: 16 hrs.
