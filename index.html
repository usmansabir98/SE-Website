Why Software Engineering?

Software Engineers are in high demand and NED University is one of the best universities of Pakistan to offer a Bacherlors in Software Engineering.
As a software engineer, you can use your expertise in computer science, engineering, and math to design, develop, and evaluate software for companies. Specific tasks software engineers perform evolve quickly, reflecting changes in technology, as well as the needs of employers. They work as members of teams that may include engineering, marketing, manufacturing, and design professionals who work together to create new products.
